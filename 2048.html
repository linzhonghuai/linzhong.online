2048

Classic number puzzle game

Game Rules

  • 🎮 Use arrow keys or swipe to control the game
  • 🔢 When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one
  • 💪 After each move, a new tile with value 2 or 4 appears
  • 🏆 The goal is to create a tile with the number 2048

Game Tips

  • ⚔️ Keep your largest numbers in the corners
  • 🛡️ Try to maintain movement in one direction
  • 📈 Prioritize merging larger numbers
  • 🎯 Avoid scattering small numbers across the board
