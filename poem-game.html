Chinese Poetry Game

Learn Chinese poetry through an interactive game

Game Rules

  • 🎮 Connect scattered Chinese characters by clicking or dragging
  • 📜 Each poem is connected line by line, automatically moving to the next line when correct
  • 💪 The game displays the title and author of each poem
  • 🏆 The goal is to correctly connect the entire poem

Game Features

  • ⚔️ Includes 15 classic Chinese poems
  • 🛡️ Characters can be freely dragged
  • 📈 Click on selected characters to deselect them
  • 🎯 Educational and fun, learn traditional Chinese culture
