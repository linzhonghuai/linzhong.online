Rise Up

Protect the balloon as it rises through obstacles

Game Features

  • 🎮 Simple yet challenging gameplay
  • 🌍 Protect your balloon from various obstacles
  • 💪 Test your reflexes and precision
  • 🔥 Increasingly difficult levels

Game Guide

  • ⚔️ Use your shield to protect the balloon
  • 🛡️ Move your shield around the balloon to block obstacles
  • 📈 The higher you go, the harder it gets
  • 🎯 Aim for the highest score possible
